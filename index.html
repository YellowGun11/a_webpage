Absolute Beachfront Apartments

Experience the ultimate Noosa holiday at Seahaven Noosa, located with direct access to Noosa Main Beach overlooking Laguna Bay. Located on the world famous Hastings Street with its renowned restaurants, cafes and boutique shopping you can enjoy the very best of Noosa at your door. For a beachfront holiday in Noosa, the perfect place to stay and relax is Seahaven Resort overlooking the sparkling Laguna Bay.

The resort has undergone a complete makeover, now offering a sophisticated modern atmosphere, designed to accommodate the requirements of families, couples and corporates working or holidaying in the region. The contemporary design with luxurious furnishings and natural finishes create a feeling of relaxed sophistication, serenity and understated luxury accommodation. With absolute beachfront access to Noosa's Main Beach and street access to Hastings Street, Seahaven Resort provides a range of holiday accommodation for couples and families in not only one of the best locations Noosa has to offer, but also great service.
